Welcome to My Website

My name is Rodrigo Aldrey and I’m currently a senior at Northeastern University studying Computer Science and Business Administration. My parents are from Venezuela, which has had a big influence on how I see the world and my interest in traveling. I’ve always enjoyed exploring new places and cultures, and traveling has become one of my favorite things to do whenever I have the opportunity. Sports have also played a major role in my life. I trained in mixed martial arts for about thirteen years, with a strong focus on Brazilian jiu-jitsu. That experience taught me a lot about discipline, persistence, and staying composed in difficult situations. During high school I also rowed competitively for several years, which pushed me physically and mentally in a completely different way. At Northeastern I’ve focused on the intersection of technology and business. Through the co-op program I’ve had the opportunity to work as a software engineering intern at an investment firm, a data analyst at a private equity firm, and most recently as a software engineering intern at JPMorgan. Outside of school and work, I love being outdoors. I’m an advanced certified scuba diver, enjoy skiing whenever I can, and generally try to spend as much time exploring the world as possible
